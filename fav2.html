Parque Güell is a public park in Barcelona, Spain, also designed by the architect Antoni Gaudí. It showcases his distinctive style of colorful mosaic work, and imaginative design.

The Camp Nou is a renowned football stadium located in Barcelona, Spain, serving as the home ground for FC Barcelona.
