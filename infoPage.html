Toursphere is a website designed to provide the best places to visit in Barcelona, made for tourists and non-tourists.

Find the places you can't miss

Toursphere will recommend the best areas of Barcelona so you can enjoy your trip to its maximum splendor. We will also show the best nearby restaurants in each area and the best points of interest.

Recommended for you

La Sagrada Familia

The Sagrada Familia is a Catholic basilica located in Barcelona, Spain, designed by the renowned Catalan architect Antoni Gaudí.

Parque Güell

Parque Güell is a public park in Barcelona, Spain, also designed by the architect Antoni Gaudí. It showcases his distinctive style of colorful mosaic work, and imaginative design.

Camp Nou

The Camp Nou is a renowned football stadium located in Barcelona, Spain, serving as the home ground for FC Barcelona.
